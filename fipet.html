Fipet Paper Co.

OVERVIEW

Founded in 2016, Fipet is primarily a stationary and logistics company with a wide range of quality and affordable stationary items. While Fipet already had an automated website using outdated interface and overly complex checkout flow, It needed a bit more customized functionalities and interactions.

Duration

3 Weeks

Focus

UX Research & Design

Target

Corporate bodies and individuals

My Role

Working together with the marketing team to gather user feedbacks through surveys, and map out layouts that solves user pain points.

The Challenge

Limitation on changing key user interface because it would interrupt the backend data.

The Solution

Maintaining a grid style layout throughout the site, making it easy to adjust backend configuration. Keeping a minimal amount of pages so user don't get tired or feel like they're not making any progress.

RESEARCH PROCESS

User Personas & User Flows

Feedback was gathered by conducting an online survey for public and private working individuals and students.

Summary of survey data:

  • Complex checkout page(s)
  • Little or no control over product preference
  • There should be seamless flow from homepage to checkout
